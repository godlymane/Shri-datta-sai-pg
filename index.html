      FAQ

      Common Questions

      What is the minimum stay period? +
      The lock-in period is 30 days. After that, you can leave with a 15-day prior notice.
      Is food included in the rent? +
      Yes, three home-cooked meals (breakfast, lunch & dinner) are included in the rent. No extra charges.
      What about the security deposit? +
      Security deposit is ₹3,000. Out of this, ₹1,000 is deducted as a one-time maintenance charge. The remaining ₹2,000 is refundable when you vacate.
      Is there a curfew or entry timing? +
      We understand professionals have different schedules. There are flexible timings with 24/7 secure access to the building.
      Can I visit before booking? +
      Absolutely! We encourage a free property tour. Call us at +91 78756 68666 or just walk in during the day.
      Is WiFi available in rooms? +
      Yes, high-speed WiFi is available in every room. We maintain consistent connectivity for work-from-home and streaming.
      Are guests/visitors allowed? +
      Visitors are allowed during daytime hours in the common area. Overnight guests are not permitted for security reasons.
      Is there an elevator? +
      Yes! We have a modern elevator with access to all floors — a rare luxury in Hinjawadi PGs.
